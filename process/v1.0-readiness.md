# v1.0 Readiness — per-Book gate tracker

*Status: Living document · Non-normative (operational tracker). · Governs nothing; it **reports** readiness against the [ROADMAP](../ROADMAP.md) Phase 2 gate and the [interim-acceptance ledger](interim-acceptance-ledger.md).*

> This document operationalizes the **ROADMAP Phase 2 v1.0 gate** so readiness is visible per Book rather than asserted globally. It does not lower or alter the gate. The gate's exit criteria (ROADMAP): **`spec/` tagged v1.0 · two independent reviewers sign off per Book · no open blocking objections.** The ledger adds: **a Book with any `pending` ledger entry touching it cannot pass.** A Book is `v1.0-ready` only when *all* of its columns are satisfied.

## Project-wide preconditions (block *every* Book)

These are not per-Book; until each is true, **no** Book can pass, regardless of its own row.

| # | Precondition | State (2026-07-22) | Why it blocks |
|---|--------------|--------------------|---------------|
| P1 | RFC-0012 (the interim process) is **`Accepted`** under its own §4.2–4.5 | ❌ `Proposed` (merged into `main` in [#18](https://github.com/dhiraj-salian/sankalpa-spec/pull/18), but not yet Accepted; cooling-off + independent pass outstanding) | The ledger-clearance mechanism the gate depends on is *defined by* RFC-0012; a gate can't run on rules not yet in force. |
| P2 | ≥1 **independent, seated Reviewer** exists (per the §4.5 independence standard) | ❌ zero Reviewers; every Domain Lead vacant ([`MAINTAINERS.md`](../MAINTAINERS.md)) | The gate needs *two independent reviewers per Book*; the founding maintainer is barred from clearing their own backlog (RFC-0012 §4.5). |
| P3 | No **open blocking objection** at project level | ⚠️ the adversarial review of RFC-0012 is resolved in code but RFC-0012 itself is not yet Accepted (see P1) | ROADMAP: "no open blocking objections." |

**Consequence:** with P1–P3 unmet, the table below is uniformly `not ready`. It exists to make the *remaining* work legible, not to imply any Book is close.

## Legend

- **Ledger** — ✅ no `pending` entry touches this Book · ❌ at least one `pending` entry (grandfathered class or RFC-0012 entries) touches it.
- **Rev 1 / Rev 2** — independent seated reviewer sign-off recorded (name + date), per RFC-0012 §4.5. `—` = none.
- **Blocking** — open blocking objections against *this Book's content* (distinct from project-wide P3). `none known` = none recorded; not a positive assurance until a reviewer looks.
- **Ready** — ✅ only if Ledger ✅ **and** Rev 1 **and** Rev 2 **and** Blocking = none **and** P1–P3 met.

*Every Book's Ledger column is ❌ because the entire corpus is entered in the ledger as the `pending` **grandfathered class** (RFCs 0001–0011, ADRs 0000–0002, Books 00–15) — accepted under pre-RFC-0012 solo practice with no compensating controls. Clearing a Book means an independent reviewer re-reviews the artifacts touching it and moves those ledger rows to `cleared`.*

## How a Book becomes ready (the actual work)

1. **Seat independent reviewers** (satisfies P2) — the hard, non-solo-substitutable step. Until this happens, nothing below is reachable.
2. **Clear the Book's ledger entries** — the seated reviewer(s) re-review the grandfathered artifacts touching the Book; each cleared row records reviewer + date. Two *distinct* independent reviewers per Book (RFC-0012 §4.5).
3. **Resolve any blocking objection** the re-review surfaces — the Phase 2 hardening batches (RFCs 0005–0011) show this is not hypothetical: independent-style review has already retracted false claims in Books 03, 05, 06, 11, 14.
4. **Flip Ready to ✅** here and, once *all* Books are ✅ and P1–P3 are met, tag `spec/` v1.0 and record it in [`../CHANGELOG.md`](../CHANGELOG.md) + [`versioning-and-stability.md`](versioning-and-stability.md).

## Honest note

This tracker cannot itself be filled in by the founding maintainer acting alone — that is the point of P2 and RFC-0012 §4.5. Its value now is to state, without euphemism, that v1.0 is gated on people who do not yet exist, and to give those people a ready-made worklist when they arrive. Pre-v1.0, a `0.x` tag (which permits breaking MINORs, [`versioning-and-stability.md`](versioning-and-stability.md)) is the honest release marker for the current Draft-complete / Phase-2-in-progress state.
